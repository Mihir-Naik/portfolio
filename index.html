About Me

My Name is Mihir Naik, and I am a Web Developer working in technologies like: Javascript, Node.js, Express, React, MongoDB, Ruby, Ruby on Rails. I enjoy programming and building things around internet to make life easy for everybody. Making a career transition move from Food Technology to coding was the best decision I ever made.

Before being a Web Developer, I used to be a Quality Assurance Engineer in Food manufacturing industry. Being a QA Engineer taught me to prioritize tasks along with problem solving and to develop a strong attention to detail.

Currently, I am wrapping up my most recent project called Querify which is a full stack application built using MERN Stack. Querify is a Q&A application which brings community together to share the knowledge along with blog posts and comments as bonus. It is designed to combine Questions & Answers app and platform for your Blogs. User can share knowledge by asking questions, responding to others' questions, reading and writing some blogs as well as commenting on others' blogs. And as a user, you can vote the answers and like or dislike the blogs. User can also edit their profile, delete account or even delete their content.
